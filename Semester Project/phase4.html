Moving forward


Narrative:

Phase 4 is when things are looking positive and you have landed employment and are able to support yourself and are prepared to help your family. This phase is a dangerous one if you have reached success because many tend to forget that with one wrong move or a work layoff could set you back drastically. This phase is when you begin to budget and plan for the future. If there is one word that is most important in this phase it would be SAVE.
